Prescribing Information

Indications:
Pharyngitis/Tonsillitis, Otitis media, Typhoid fever, Pneumonia, Acute bronchitis, Acute Exacerbations of Chronic Bronchitis (AECB), Urinary Tract Infection, Uncomplicated Gonorrhea
Dosage & Administration:
Capsule: 1 or 2 capsule as a single dose or in two divided doses daily for 7 to 14 days, according to the severity of infection.
Suspension: 8 mg / kg daily as a single dose or in two divided doses daily for 7 to 14 days, according to the severity of infection or
for children of age ½ - 1 year: 3.75 ml or 75 mg,
1 – 4 years : 5 ml or 100 mg,
5 – 10 years : 10 ml or 200 mg,
11 – 12 years : 15 ml or 300 mg and
above 12 years : Adult dose-Typhoid Fever: 20 mg/kg/day in two divided doses
Use in pregnancy & Lactation :The safety of Cefixime in pregnancy has not been established. The drug should be prescribed during pregnancy only if clearly needed
