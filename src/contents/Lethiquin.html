Prescribing Information

Indications:
Acute maxillary sinusitis 
Acute bacterial exacerbation.
Complicated urinary tract infections 
Acute pyelonephritis 
Uncomplicated & complicated skin and soft tissue infections

Dosage & Administration:
Acute sinusitis: 500 mg once daily for 10-14 days
Acute bacterial exacerbation of chronic bronchitis: 250-500 mg daily for 7 days
Community-acquired pneumonia: 500 mg once or twice daily for 7-14 days
Complicated urinary-tract infections and acute pyelonephritis: 250 mg daily for 7-10 days
Uncomplicated skin and soft-tissue infections: 500 mg once daily for 7-10 days
Complicated skin and soft-issue infections: 750 mg once daily for 7-10 days
Use in pregnancy & lactation:Levofloxacin is not recommended for use during pregnancy or nursing, as the effects on the unborn child or nursing infant are unknown.
