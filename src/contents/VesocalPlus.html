Prescribing Information

Indications: Hypertension is not controlled by monotherapy, angina pectoris and hypertension are co-exist, post MI patients, refractory Angina pectoris where nitrate therapy has failed.
Dosage & Administration:
Usually initiated with a single dose of Amlodipine 5 mg + Atenolol 50 mg. depending upon the therapeutic response, titration of the dosage is recommended
Use in children: Use of Amlodipine in children (under 12 years of age) is not recommended.
Use in pregnancy & lactation:The combination should be used during pregnancy only if the expected benefit outweighs the potential fetal risk.
