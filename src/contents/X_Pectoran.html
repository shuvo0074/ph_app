Prescribing Information

Indications: X-pectoran is indicated in the treatment of respiratory disorders associated with productive cough. These include; tracheobronchitis, bronchitis with emphysema, bronchiectasis, bronchitis with bronchospasm, chronic inflammatory pulmonary conditions and pneumoconiosis
Dosage & Administration:
X-pectoran tablet:
Adults and children over 10 years:
8-16 mg 3 times daily Children 5-10 years: 4 mg 3 times daily.
X-pectoran syrup:
Adults :
The recommended daily dose is 2 to 4 teaspoonful 3 times. Initially 4 teaspoonful 3 times daily and then as required
Children: Suggested dosage for children under 2 years is ¼ teaspoonful 3 times daily, for 2-5 years ½ teaspoonful 3 times daily and for children aged 5-10 years 1 teaspoonful 3 times daily
Preparation:
X – pectoran Tablet : 5 x 10’s in blister pack
X – pectoran syrup : Bottle containing 60 ml / 100 ml syrup
