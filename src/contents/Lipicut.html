Prescribing Information

Indications: Lipicut® is used to lower lipids known as cholesterol and triglycerides in the blood when a low fat diet and life style changes on their own have failed. If you are at an increased risk of heart disease, Lipicut® can also be used to reduce such risk even if your cholesterol levels are normal
Dosage & Administration: The usual starting dose of Lipicut® is 10 mg once a day in adults and children aged 10 years or older. This may be increased if necessary by your doctor until you are taking the amount you need. Your doctor will adapt the dose at intervals of 4 weeks or more. The maximum dose of Lipicut® is 80 mg once daily for adults and 20 mg once daily for children
Use in pregnancy :The safety of Lipicut® during pregnancy and breast-feeding has not yet been proven. Ask your doctor or pharmacist for advice before taking any medicine
