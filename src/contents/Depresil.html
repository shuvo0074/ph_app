Prescribing Information

Indications: Anxiety & Depression, Psychogenic affection, Functional Dyspepsia, Personality Disorder.
Dosage & Administration: Oryx®
Adults: Usually 2 tablets daily: morning and noon. In severe cases the morning dose may be increased to 2 tablets.
Elderly patients: 1 tablet in the morning.
Maintenance dose: Usually 1 tablet in the morning. In case of insomnia and severe restlessness additional treatment with sedative in the acute phase is recommended Use in pregnancy & lactation:: Flupentixol-Melitracen should preferably not be given during pregnancy and lactation
