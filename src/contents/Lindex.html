Prescribing Information

Indications: Pneumonia, acute and chronic Bronchitis, pharyngitis, tonsillitis, sinusitis, otitis media, dentoalveolar infections, abscesses, cellulitis, furunculosis, impetigo, pyelonephritis, cystitis, urethritis and gynecological infections, postoperative infections in patients undergoing surgical procedures associated with a high risk of infections
Dosage & Administration:
Usual Adult Dose :Respiratory tract infections - 250-500 mg four times daily, Urinary tract infections - 500 mg four times daily, Skin and soft tissue infections - 250-500 mg four times daily
Usual Adult Dose : The usual dose - 25-50 mg/kg/day in two or four equally divided doses, In severe infection - Up to 100 mg/kg/day in two or four equally divided doses
Usual Adult Dose : The Usual dose - 2 to 4 gm daily in 3 to 4 equally divided doses by deep intramuscular or intravenous route. Dose may be increased to 8 gm daily in severe infections
Use in pregnancy & lactation:No teratogenicity has been demonstrated in animals, but safety in pregnancy has not been established. Cefradine is excreted in the breast milk and should be used with caution in lactating mothers.
