Prescribing Information

Indications: Skin and soft tissue infections such as-cellulitis, impetigo, pyoderma, curbuncles, boils, abscesses, osteomyelitis, otitis media, otitis externa, prophylactic as well as curative agent in major surgical procedures and endocarditis.
Dosage & Administration:
The usual dose is 250 mg 6 hourly. Depending on severity the dose may be doubled. Doses should be administered 1 hour before meals.
Use in pregnancy & lactation: Penicillins are generally considered safe for use in pregnancy. Animal studies with Flucloxacillin have shown no teratogenic effects
