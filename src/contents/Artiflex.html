Prescribing Information

Indications: The combination of chondroitin and glucosamine is widely used to aid in maintaining healthy joints. It is also used as a nutritional supplement in people with osteoarthritis or other inflammatory joint disorders
Dosage & Administration:
For treatment: 1 to 2 tablets three times daily or as directed by the physician. Obese individuals may need higher doses, based on body weight.
For prevention (as dietary supplement): One tablet three times daily
Usually, positive changes occur in one to two months. After two months you may start reducing the number of tablets by the direction of physician
Use in pregnancy & Lactation : Pregnancy category C. Women who are pregnant or who could become pregnant should not supplement with Glucosamine and Chondroitin. They have not been studied enough to determine their effects on a developing fetus, and no studies have evaluated the use of Glucosamine and Chondroitin during pregnancy or lactation
