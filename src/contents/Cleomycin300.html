Prescribing Information

Indications: Skin and soft tissue infections, dental infections, C. trachomatis cervicitis, mild, moderate and severe diabetic foot infections.
Dosage & Administration:
Adults: Severe Infections: 150 mg - 300 mg every six hours
More severe infections: 300 mg - 450 mg every six hours
Use in pregnancy & lactation:Pregnancy Category B: Clindamycin crosses the placenta in humans. After multiple doses, amniotic fluid concentrations were approximately 30% of maternal concentrations. Clindamycin should be used in pregnancy only if clearly needed
