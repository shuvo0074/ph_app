Prescribing Information

Indications:
Stimulation of gut mobility: Non-ulcer dyspepsia, Esophageal reflux, reflux esophagitis and gastritis, Diabetic gastroparesis and Functional dyspepsia. Prevention and symptomatic relief of acute nausea and vomiting from any cause including cytotoxic therapy, radio therapy and anti-parkinsonism therapy. In the treatment of migraine
Dosage & Administration:
The recommended oral dose for
Adults : 10 - 20 mg every 4 - 8 hours daily
Children : 0.2 - 0.4 mg/kg every 4 - 8 hours daily.
Note : Omidon tablet and suspension should be taken 15 - 30 minutes before a meal.
For acute nausea and vomiting, maximum period of treatment is 12 weeks
Use in pregnancy & Lactation :The safety of Domperidone has not been proven and it is therefore not recommended during pregnancy.Animal studies have not demonstrated teratogenic effects on the fetus
