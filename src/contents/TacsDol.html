Prescribing Information

Indications:
Moderate to severe pain, Acute postoperative pain, Traumatic pain, Acute low back pain, Chronic Cancer pain, Gynecological pain
Dosage & Administration: Adults:: The recommended dose is 1 or 2 tablets every 4 to 6 hours as needed for pain relief up to a maximum of 8 tablets daily
Use in pregnancy & Lactation : US FDA Pregnancy Category C. There are no adequate and well-controlled studies in pregnant women. This tablet should be used during pregnancy only if the potential benefit justifies the potential risk to the fetus. This tablet is not recommended for obstetrical preoperative medication or for post-delivery analgesia in nursing mothers because it’s safety in infants and newborns has not been studied
