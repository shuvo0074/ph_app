Prescribing Information

Indications: Montelukast is indicated for the- Prophylaxis & chronic treatment of asthma in adults & pediatric patients 12 months of age & older, relief of symptoms of allergic rhinitis
Dosage & Administration:
Adults and adolescents 15 years of age : 10 mg once daily.
Pediatric patients 6 to 14 years of age: 5 mg once daily
Use in pregnancy : Animal studies do not indicate harmful effects with respect to effects on pregnancy or embryonal/foetal development. Limited data from available pregnancy databases do not suggest a causal relationship between Montelukast and malformations (i.e. limb defects) that have been rarely reported in worldwide post-marketing experience. Montelukast may be used during pregnancy only if it is considered to be clearly essential
