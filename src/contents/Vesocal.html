Prescribing Information

Indications: Patients with mild to moderate hypertension (alone or in combination with other anti-hypertensive). The treatment of chronic stable and vesospastic angina.
Dosage & Administration:
For treatment of both hypertension and angina pectoris, the usual initial dose is 5 mg once daily. If the desired therapeutic effect cannot be achieved within 2-4 weeks, the dose may be increased to a maximum dose of 10 mg once daily.
Use in children: Use of Amlodipine in children (under 12 years of age) is not recommended.
Use in pregnancy & lactation:Safety in pregnancy has not been established.
