Prescribing Information

Indications: Hypertension, Chronic heart failure and it is also indicated for the reduction of renal disease progression in patients with type 2 Diabetes
Dosage & Administration:
The usual starting and maintenance dose is 50 mg once daily for most patients. Some patients may receive an additional benefit by increasing the dose to 100 mg
Use in pregnancy & lactation:Losartan Potassium must be discontinued as soon as possible when pregnancy is detected. It should not be prescribed during lactation as there is no information in humans on the passage of Losartan into breast milk
