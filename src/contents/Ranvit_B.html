Prescribing Information

Indications:
It is indicated for the prevention and treatment of vitamin B-Complex deficiency states, such as during pregnancy, lactation prolonged antibiotic therapy, convalescence and prophylaxis
Dosage & Administration:
Tablet : 1-2 tablets three times daily or as directed by the physician. Syrup : Adult: 1-2 teaspoonful three times daily. Children: 1 teaspoonful three times daily or as directed by registered physician
Use in pregnancy & Lactation :The safety of Cefixime in pregnancy has not been established. The drug should be prescribed during pregnancy only if clearly needed
