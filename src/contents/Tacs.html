Prescribing Information

Indications:
relief of pain and discomfort associated with headache, tension headache, migraine headache, osteoarthritis, arthritis, cold & flu symptoms, toothache, dental procedures, muscular aches, sore throat and period pain. Reduces fever
Dosage & Administration: Adults : 1-2 tablets every 4-6 hours. Maximum dose: 8 tablets daily. Not recommended for children below 12 years
Children:Children under 10 kg : 2 teaspoonful syrup daily after food  Children within 10-30 kg : 2 teaspoonful syrup daily after food 1-3 times daily Children over 30 kg : 4 teaspoonful syrup daily after food 1-3 times daily
Use in pregnancy & Lactation : Although there is epidemiological evidence of the safety of paracetamol in pregnancy and lactation, medical advice should be sought before using this product
