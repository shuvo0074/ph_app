Dosage & Administration:
The daily intake Vitamin E in the range of 200 - 600 mg is considered safe. The recommended dose is one capsule once daily with or after meals or as prescribed by the physician
Use in pregnancy :Vitamin E is safe in pregnancy and lactation, when used as recommended doses. Higher doses are not established
