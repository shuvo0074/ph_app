Children: Children over 6 months 10 mg/kg once daily for 3 days; or body weight 15-25 kg, 200 mg once daily for 3 days; or body weight 26-35 kg, 300 mg once daily for 3 days; or body weight 36-45 kg, 400 mg once daily for 3 days; As common with may other antibiotics Azithromycin should be taken at least 1 hour before or 2 hours after meal and antacids Use in pregnancy & lactation:US FDA pregnancy category B: Animal reproduction studies have failed to demonstrate a risk to the fetus and there are no adequate and well-controlled studies in pregnant women.
