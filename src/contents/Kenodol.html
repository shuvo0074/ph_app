Prescribing Information

Indications:KENODOL(Ketorolac Tromethamine) is indicated for the short-term management of moderate to moderately severe acute pain, including post-surgical pain (such as general, orthopaedic and dental surgery), acute musculoskeletal trauma pain and post-partum uterine cramping pain.
Dosage & Administration:The usual oral dose of Ketorolac Tromethamine is 10 mg every 4 to 6 hours as required. Doses exceeding 40 mg per day are not recommended.
