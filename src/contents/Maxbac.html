Prescribing Information

Indications:
Lower Respiratory tract infections, Urinary tract infections, Meningitis, Pneumonia, Peritonitis, Skin & skin structure infections, Severe Nosocomial infection, Severe infection in ICU patients
Dosage & Administration:

Adults:: The usual dosage is 1 to 6 g per day 8 or 12 hourly (IM/IV). In the majority of infections, 1g 8 hourly or 2 g 12 hourly should be given.
Neonates (Aged 0-28 days): IV : 30 mg/kg every 12 hours
Children (1 Month to 12 Years of Age): IV : 30-50 mg/kg every 8 hours (up to 6 g daily).
Use in pregnancy & Lactation :Pregnancy Category B. It should be used during pregnancy only if clearly needed

Preparation:
Maxbac ® 250 mg IV/IM : 250 mg Ceftazidime (as Ceftazidime Pentahydrate) + 5 ml Water for Injection.
Maxbac ® 500 mg IV/IM : 500 mg Ceftazidime (as Ceftazidime Pentahydrate) + 5 ml Water for Injection.

Maxbac ® 1 g IV/IM : 1 g Ceftazidime (as Ceftazidime Pentahydrate) + 10 ml Water for Injection.
Additional Accessory: Each pack contains - A sterile disposable syringe with an extra needle , An alcohol prep pad , A strip bandage
